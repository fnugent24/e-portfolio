Reflection

For this unit, I am learning about UML (Unified Modelling Languages). I found the lecturecast to be very helpful when giving a clear and organised way of how UML works and the different models that are involved within in. I feel confident in understanding what the different models do, which models are associated with which type (behavioural, structural or architectural), and why there are different models to be aware of.
